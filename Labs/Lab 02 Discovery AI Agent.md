# 🧪 Lab 02 Building the Market Trend Discovery AI Agent


## Objective:
Developed an autonomous AI agent pipeline designed to bridge the gap between raw market data and actionable business strategy. This project focuses on solving "context contamination" in unstructured data by applying rigorous multi-step AI reasoning to live-scraped product and social trends.

## Core Functionality:

- **Autonomous Data Sourcing:** Built custom scrapers to aggregate real-time product data from Amazon and trend signals from various social media and industry design blogs.

- **Multi-Model Intelligence:** Integrated a multi-LLM architecture using Mistral for data normalization and Google Gemini for high-level insight synthesis and report generation.

- **Hardware-Aware Engineering:** Optimized the workflow to handle complex AI reasoning steps and visual generation (via Hugging Face) while maintaining efficient resource management.

- **Automated Reporting:** The pipeline concludes with the generation of a dynamic, interactive HTML Trend Report, providing stakeholders with instant visibility into emerging micro-segments and market risks.
